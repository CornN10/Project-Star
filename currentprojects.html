Current Projects

Our current projects include:
  • Built and redecorated 5 multi sensory rooms around Thailand, with the most recent example being at CCD
  • 10 scholarships for disabled and disadvantaged students to pursue higher education
  • Donated 2 prosthetic limbs to hospitals around Bangkok
  • Finding jobs for disabled individuals by connecting our partners (CCD, TCDF, and Vulcan Coalition) to one another, so CCD and TCDF have workers that will be employed at Vulcan.
  • A trip to Vulcan Coalition headquarters
